Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy is written in plain English, not legal jargon. HireEU is a small early-stage project — there's no team of lawyers here, just one person building something useful. Here's exactly what happens with your data, honestly.

📋 What data we collect

We collect one thing only when you join the waitlist:

Email address The email you type into the waitlist form
Signup timestamp The date and time you submitted the form

That's it. No name, no location, no tracking cookies, no analytics scripts. We don't use Google Analytics or any third-party trackers on this page.

🎯 Why we collect it

One reason only: to email you when HireEU launches and you can get early access.

We will never use your email for anything else — no newsletters you didn't ask for, no marketing from partners, nothing unrelated to HireEU.

👤 Who can see your data

Only the founder of HireEU. No team, no contractors, no third parties have access to the waitlist.

The only exception is Neon (our database provider) who stores the data on their servers — but they cannot read or use it, they just provide the storage infrastructure.

🚫 Do we sell or share your data

No. Never. Your email address will never be sold, rented, shared, or given to any other company or person for any reason.

🌍 Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on servers located in Frankfurt, Germany (EU), provided by Neon.tech.

This means your data stays within the European Union and is subject to EU data protection standards (GDPR).

🗑️ How to request deletion

You can ask us to delete your email from the waitlist at any time, no questions asked.

Just send an email to hireeuapp@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my data" and we'll remove your email within 48 hours and confirm when it's done.

⚖️ Your rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU or EEA, you have the right to:

Access Ask what data we hold about you
Deletion Ask us to delete your data (see above)
Correction Ask us to correct your email if it was entered wrong
Portability Ask for a copy of your data in a readable format

To exercise any of these, just email hireeuapp@gmail.com.

📝 Changes to this policy

If anything changes about how we handle data, we'll update this page and change the "last updated" date at the top. We won't notify you by email unless the change is significant.

✉️ Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hireeuapp@gmail.com — you'll get a reply from a real person, not a bot.
